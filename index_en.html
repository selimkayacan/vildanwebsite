SAP & S/4HANA Transformation

SAP S/4HANA transitions are complex transformation programs requiring the simultaneous management of strategy, architecture, data, organization, and governance. I have accompanied institutions such as THY, Eureko Sigorta, Arçelik, and Yapı Kredi on this journey.

Scope: Transition strategy, RFP/vendor selection, program management, target operating model, go-live & stabilization.

Managed Services & Operating Model Design

Over 10+ years at NTT Data, I designed, built, and managed ITIL-based service models for large enterprises across EMEA and META. For B/S/H, I stood up a 75+ person service organization in three months.

Scope: Service model design, SLA/KPI framework, organizational structure, governance, transition planning.

Vendor Selection & RFP Management

Technology vendors come with strong sales teams. As an independent evaluator, I address the technical, commercial, and organizational dimensions together to help you make the best decision.

Scope: RFP/RFI design, evaluation criteria & weighting, independent assessment report, negotiation support.

Program & Portfolio Management

At NTT Data, I established and led the PMO. At SAP Turkey, I managed the country's largest and most complex transformation programs — including THY, Arçelik, and Eureko Sigorta — on time and on budget.

Scope: PMO setup, portfolio management, governance frameworks, program leadership.

AI Adoption & Change Management

I bring both deep enterprise transformation experience and the perspective of a practitioner who actively builds and uses AI-enabled tools. I have built AI-powered project management systems in my own practice.

Scope: AI readiness assessment, strategy, change management, workforce readiness, governance.

Enterprise Architecture & Governance

As a TOGAF-certified enterprise architect, I bring a holistic approach to aligning technology architecture with business strategy. I designed governance frameworks adopted enterprise-wide at NTT Data and SAP Turkey.

Scope: Architecture assessment, target architecture design, governance model, risk management.
