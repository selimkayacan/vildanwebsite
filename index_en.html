My Services

Independent advisory across every phase of transformation

SAP & S/4HANA Transformation

SAP S/4HANA transitions are complex transformation programs requiring the simultaneous management of strategy, architecture, data, organization, and governance. I have accompanied institutions such as THY, Eureko Sigorta, Arçelik, and Yapı Kredi on this journey — on your side, for your interests.

Scope: Transition strategy, RFP/vendor selection, program management, target operating model, go-live & stabilization.

Managed Services & Operating Model Design

Over 10+ years at NTT Data, I designed, built, and managed ITIL-based service models for large enterprises across EMEA and META. For B/S/H, I stood up a 75+ person service organization in three months, meeting SLAs from day one.

Scope: Service model design, SLA/KPI framework, organizational structure, governance, transition planning, continuous improvement.

Vendor Selection & RFP Management

Technology vendors come with strong sales teams. As an independent evaluator, I address the technical, commercial, and organizational dimensions together. I now bring my NTT Data RFP experience to the client side — working for your interests.

Scope: RFP/RFI design, evaluation criteria & weighting, independent assessment report, negotiation support.

Program & Portfolio Management

At NTT Data I established and led the PMO. At SAP Turkey I managed the country's largest transformation programs — THY, Arçelik, Eureko Sigorta. I consistently delivered €1M+ programs on time and on budget.

Scope: PMO setup, portfolio management & prioritization, governance frameworks, program/transformation leadership, health monitoring & reporting.

AI Adoption & Change Management

I bring both deep enterprise transformation experience and the perspective of a practitioner who actively builds and uses AI-enabled tools. I have built AI-powered project management systems in my own practice. Buying technology is not enough — preparing the organization is what matters.

Scope: AI readiness assessment, strategy & roadmap, change management, workforce readiness, AI governance framework, pilot program management.

Enterprise Architecture & Governance

As a TOGAF-certified enterprise architect, I bring a holistic approach to aligning technology architecture with business strategy. At NTT Data and SAP Turkey I designed governance and delivery frameworks adopted enterprise-wide.

Scope: Architecture assessment, target architecture design, governance model design, risk & quality management frameworks, architecture principles.
